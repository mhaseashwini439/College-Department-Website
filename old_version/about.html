About Our Institution

A Legacy of Excellence in Higher Education since 1970

Our Glorious History

Established in 1970, New Arts, Commerce and Science College, Ahmednagar, was founded with the vision of providing quality higher education to the rural and urban youth of the region. Over the past five decades, it has grown into one of the premier autonomous educational institutions in Maharashtra.

The college is run by the Ahmednagar Jilha Maratha Vidya Prasarak Samaj, a pioneering educational trust committed to social upliftment through education. With its A++ accreditation by NAAC, the college continues to set benchmarks in academic and holistic development.

Our Vision

To build a sustainable society through quality education, character building, and innovative research, empowering the youth to lead global challenges.

Our Mission

To provide excellent academic environment, foster overall development, and instill ethical values in students, making them responsible citizens.

NAAC Status & Accreditation

Accredited with 'A++' Grade by NAAC (CGPA: 3.79)

This prestigious accreditation reflects our commitment to maintaining high standards in teaching, learning, research, and infrastructure. It is a testament to the hard work of our faculty, staff, and students.
