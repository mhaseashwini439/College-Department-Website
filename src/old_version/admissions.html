Admission Process

Simple 4-step process to secure your seat at New Arts College

1

Online Registration

Register yourself on the admission portal and verify your email/mobile.

2

Fill Application

Complete the online application form with personal and academic details.

3

Upload Documents

Upload scanned copies of required documents like 10th/12th marksheets.

4

Pay Fees

Pay the admission fee online to confirm your provisional seat.

Required Documents

  • 10th & 12th Marksheets
  • Transfer Certificate (TC)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Migration Certificate (Other states)
  • Income Certificate
